           1. DESIGN    PHILOSOPHY

           Healing is not instant.

           Healing requires:

              • time;
              • resources;
              • knowledge;
              • people.

           The player must prepare for danger.

           2. HEALTH    STATES

           Characters exist in one of several states.

           Perfect Health ↓ Minor Injuries ↓ Moderate Injuries ↓ Serious Injuries ↓ Critical Condition ↓ Death

           3. TREATMENT      TIERS

           There are four levels of treatment.

           SELF TREATMENT

           The player may:

              • apply bandages;
              • drink medicine;
              • stop bleeding;
              • disinfect wounds;
