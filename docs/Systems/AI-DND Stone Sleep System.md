           Version 0.1

           1. CORE   IDEA

           The world does not stop when the player leaves.

           When the player is absent, the character enters a mysterious state known as:

           Stone Sleep

           In ancient language, such people are called:

           Mor'Talari

           Meaning:

           "Those who belong to two times."

           2. LORE   EXPLANATION

           Mor'Talari are rare cursed people.

           When Stone Sleep begins, they:

              • fall unconscious;
              • turn into stone;
              • stop aging;
              • stop bleeding;
              • stop feeling pain;
              • do not need food;
              • do not need water.

           No one knows the true cause.

3. PLAYER   ABSENCE

           When the player exits the game:

           Player Character ↓ Stone Sleep ↓ World Simulation Continues

           The world continues to change.

           4. RETURN    TO  GAME

           When the player returns:

              • the character awakens;
              • time has passed;
              • the world state is updated;
              • NPCs remember the absence.

           5. WORLD    EVENTS    DURING     ABSENCE

           During Stone Sleep, possible events include:

              • wars;
              • deaths;
              • births;
              • diseases;
              • trade changes;
              • faction conflicts;
              • rumors;
              • economic shifts;
              • settlement changes.

           6. RETURN    SUMMARY

           After awakening, the player receives a natural summary.

           Example:

           9. PROTECTION      DURING     STONE    SLEEP

           While turned to stone, the character:

              • cannot be killed normally;
              • cannot be poisoned;
              • cannot starve;
              • cannot bleed;
              • cannot age.
           However, the body may be:

              • moved;
              • hidden;
              • stolen;
              • imprisoned;
              • worshipped;
              • guarded.

           11. ABUSE    PREVENTION

           Stone Sleep must not become an exploit.

           The system may limit:

              • time compression;
              • event severity;
              • world destruction;
              • permanent losses.

           12. TIME   COMPRESSION

           Real absence does not always equal game time.

           Example:

           1 real hour:

              • several game hours.

           1 real day:

              • 1-3 game days.

           1 real week:

              • 3-10 game days.

           1 real month:

              • 2-6 game weeks.
