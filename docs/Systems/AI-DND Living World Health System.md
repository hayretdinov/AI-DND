AI-DND

           1. CORE   PHILOSOPHY

           The player is not the center of the world.

           NPCs:

              • become sick;
              • get injured;
              • recover;
              • suffer disabilities;
              • age;
              • die.

           The world continues to exist without the player.

           2. NPC  HEALTH     MODEL

           Every NPC has:

              • physical health;
              • mental health;
              • fatigue;
              • hunger;
              • diseases;
              • injuries;
              • addictions;
              • fears.

           Example:

           NPC: Blacksmith Harrod

           Status:

              • healthy

           NPCs may receive injuries from:

              • combat;
              • accidents;

              • work;

              • cuts
           Serious Injury

              • fractures
              • deep wounds

           Critical Injury

              • permanent disability
              • loss of limbs

              • healers become expensive

           14. AI NPC   KNOWLEDGE

           NPCs know only what they could realistically know.

           Example:

           Village farmer knows:

              • local illnesses
              • local deaths
              • local rumors

           Village farmer does not know:

              • political secrets
              • distant wars
              • hidden locations
