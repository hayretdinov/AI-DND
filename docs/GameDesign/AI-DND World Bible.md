           1. THE  WORLD

           Name  of the World

           ELYRION

           Elyrion is an ancient world that has experienced multiple rises and falls of civilization.

           Most inhabitants believe that they live in the final age of the world.

           No one knows whether this is true.

           2. THE  CONTINENT

           Name

           VALGAR

           Valgar is a northern continent known for:

              • long winters;
              • dense forests;
              • rich mineral deposits;
              • ancient ruins;
              • constant political conflicts.

           Most of the game takes place within one isolated region of Valgar.

3. THE  GAME    REGION

           Name

           THE ASHEN VALLEY

           The Ashen Valley is a massive basin surrounded by mountains.

           For hundreds of years, nobody has been able to leave it.

           The valley became isolated after a magical catastrophe known as:

           THE FRACTURE

           4. THE  FRACTURE

           Approximately 400 years ago, a war between ancient magical orders reached its climax.

           The archmages attempted to create a barrier capable of stopping an unknown enemy.

           The ritual failed.

           Instead of creating protection, it created:

           THE FRACTURE.

           The Fracture surrounds the entire valley.

           Characteristics:

              • glowing storms;
              • floating rocks;
              • distorted space;
              • whispering voices;
              • lethal magical energy.

           Any living being attempting to cross the Fracture disappears.

           No bodies have ever been recovered.

5. WHY   PEOPLE    STAY

           Inside the Ashen Valley exists a rare substance:

           BLACK CRYSTAL

           Properties:

              • stores magical energy;
              • powers magical artifacts;
              • enables advanced spellcasting;
              • is extremely valuable.
           Without Black Crystal, many kingdoms outside the valley would collapse.

           Therefore governments continue to send:

              • soldiers;
              • prisoners;
              • scholars;
              • mercenaries;
              • adventurers;
              • spies.
